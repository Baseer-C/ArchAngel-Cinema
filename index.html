07 / QUESTIONS, ANSWERED

Know what happens
before the date is yours.

Where does ArchAngel work?

ArchAngel serves the Washington, D.C., Maryland, and Northern Virginia region. Travel, parking, permits, and unusual venue requirements are disclosed in the quote.

How quickly are films delivered?

Most highlight and promotional deliverables arrive within 14 business days. Legacy full-length edits may take up to 20 business days. Preview and rush options depend on the collection.

How is speech and performance audio captured?

We plan professional primary and backup audio around the featured speech, program, performance, or ceremony. The exact signal path depends on the venue and production.

When do you bring another camera or operator?

When simultaneous moments, separate rooms, schedule risk, or uninterrupted key coverage require it. Necessary crew and rentals are identified in the custom proposal.

Can I receive raw footage?

Raw footage starts at $400 when requested and is clearly labeled as unedited media. It does not replace the finished films in a collection.

How do revisions work?

Collections include one consolidated revision round on the creative highlight or selected deliverable. Full-length documentary edits include factual corrections rather than creative restructuring.

How is the date secured?

A signed agreement and 50% date retainer secure the event. The balance is due seven calendar days before coverage. Tentative proposal holds expire after five business days.

What happens if the event changes?

Rescheduling and cancellation terms are defined in the agreement. Contact ArchAngel as soon as possible so availability, rentals, crew, and venue changes can be reviewed.
