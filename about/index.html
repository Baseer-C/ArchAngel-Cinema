One person accountable for what cannot happen twice.

I’m Baseer, founder and filmmaker at ArchAngel Cinema. When you book ArchAngel, you work directly with me—from the first planning conversation through picture, sound, editing, and final delivery.

WHAT I DO

Plan carefully. Capture calmly. Deliver completely.

Before the date, I learn the timeline, venue, people, words, and moments that matter. During the event, I protect the picture and professional audio without becoming the center of the room. Afterward, I shape the footage and sound into films that feel honest to the day.

WHY IT MATTERS

The room empties. The meaning should remain.

A speech is spoken once. A performance ends. Reactions happen before anyone knows to look for them. Thoughtful filmmaking preserves more than how an event looked—it keeps the voices, energy, and feeling close enough to return to.

My role is to bring calm accountability to an irreplaceable day. No handoff maze and no uncertainty about who is responsible—just one filmmaker who understands the plan and stays with the story until it is delivered.
